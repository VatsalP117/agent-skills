# Design System Interview (Quick Mode)

## Q1. What are you building?
A modern AI product suite: a narrative marketing site, plus a data-heavy SaaS dashboard where users monitor model quality, usage, and releases.

## Q2. Pick 3-5 words for the feel
Precise, Sophisticated, Focused, Fast, Trustworthy.

## Q3. Share 1-2 UIs you love, and why
1. Apple product pages: cinematic typography, premium restraint, calm confidence.
2. Linear: dense but elegant task surfaces, disciplined borders, no visual noise.
3. Stripe dashboard/docs: clear forms, trustworthy hierarchy, operational polish.

## Synthesized Signals Used For This Demo
- High contrast neutral foundations with restrained but vivid accent color.
- Bold editorial display type paired with practical sans for body copy.
- Border-led components with minimal heavy shadows.
- Fast 100ms interaction rhythm with subtle lift on hover.
- Premium dark-first composition plus warm off-white light theme.
